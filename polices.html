Editorial Polices and Submissions

Editorial Polices

We accept papers on various topics in metaphysics, epistemology, philosophy of language, philosophy of science, philosophical psychology, philosophical logic and related areas. All papers are blind refereed. We will make every effort to respond with the decision regarding the acceptance of the paper as soon as possible.
